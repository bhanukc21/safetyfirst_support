Safety First ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Important: By using Safety First, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued ID, driver's license (for drivers)
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, payment history
  • Profile Data: Preferences, saved addresses, emergency contacts

1.2 Location Information

We collect precise location data when you use our services:

  • Real-time GPS location during active rides
  • Pickup and drop-off locations
  • Route and trip history
  • Background location (for drivers when app is running)

1.3 Device Information

  • Device type, operating system, and version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Mobile network information
  • App version and usage data

1.4 Usage Information

  • Trip details (date, time, duration, distance)
  • App interactions and features used
  • Communication between riders and drivers
  • Customer support interactions
  • Ratings and reviews

1.5 Emergency and Safety Information

  • Emergency contact information
  • SOS alerts and incident reports
  • Audio/video recordings (when safety features are activated)
  • Police escort requests and disaster mode activations

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Delivery

  • Connecting riders with drivers
  • Processing and completing trips
  • Calculating fares and processing payments
  • Providing customer support
  • Sending trip notifications and updates

2.2 Safety and Security

  • Verifying user identity and driver credentials
  • Monitoring trips for safety concerns
  • Responding to emergency situations
  • Investigating incidents and complaints
  • Preventing fraud and unauthorized access

2.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns and trends
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Developing new features and services
  • Conducting research and analytics

2.4 Communication

  • Sending service-related notifications
  • Providing customer support
  • Sending promotional offers (with your consent)
  • Sharing important updates and policy changes

2.5 Legal Compliance

  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Responding to legal requests
  • Protecting our rights and property
  • Enforcing our terms and policies

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 With Other Users

  • Riders can see driver name, photo, vehicle details, and location
  • Drivers can see rider name, photo, rating, and pickup/drop-off locations
  • Trip details are shared between riders and drivers

3.2 With Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:

  • Payment processors
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Analytics services
  • Customer support platforms
  • Marketing and communication tools

3.3 With Law Enforcement and Legal Authorities

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To respond to government requests
  • During emergency situations affecting safety
  • To investigate suspected fraud or violations

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

3.5 With Your Consent

We may share your information with your explicit consent for specific purposes not covered in this policy.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security assessments and audits
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Incident response procedures

Note: While we strive to protect your information, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your personal information through the app settings at any time.

5.2 Delete Account

You can request deletion of your account by contacting support@safetyfirst.com. We will retain certain information as required by law.

5.3 Location Permissions

You can control location access through your device settings. Note that disabling location services will prevent you from using our ride services.

5.4 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of promotional emails and notifications through app settings or unsubscribe links.

5.5 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a portable format by contacting us.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

Trip data is typically retained for 7 years for legal and tax purposes. Account information is retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter.
