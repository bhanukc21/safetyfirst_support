Frequently Asked Questions

For Riders

How do I book a ride?

Open the Safety First app, enter your pickup and drop-off locations, and tap "Request Ride".

How do I pay for my ride?

You can pay using cash, card, or digital wallets. Payment methods can be selected in the app.

Can I cancel my ride?

Yes, you can cancel before the driver arrives. Cancellation charges may apply if cancelled after driver acceptance.

How do I track my driver?

Once a driver accepts your ride, you can track them in real-time on the map.

What is the Disaster Mode?

During emergency situations, Disaster Mode activates to provide priority rides and ensure passenger safety.

For Drivers

How do I register as a driver?

Download the Safety First Driver app and complete the registration process with your documents.

What documents do I need?

You need a valid driver's license, vehicle registration, insurance papers, and identity proof.

How do I receive payments?

Payments are deposited directly to your linked bank account on a weekly basis.

Can I go offline?

Yes, you can toggle between online and offline modes in the driver app.

What if I face an issue during a trip?

Use the in-app emergency button or call our 24/7 support hotline immediately.

Technical Support

Experiencing technical issues with the app? Try these steps:

  • Ensure you have the latest version of the app installed
  • Check your internet connection
  • Restart the app
  • Clear app cache (in phone settings)
  • Reinstall the app if problems persist

If issues continue, contact our technical support team at jithendra@techranges.in
